# Code Of Conduct

CrashQuest is an open-source project for practical, safe troubleshooting. Contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate and professional.
- Assume good intent when reviewing issues and pull requests.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect privacy and avoid requesting account credentials, tokens, or private data.
- Keep safety concerns visible when discussing troubleshooting steps.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private user data without consent.
- Promoting unsafe fixes, malware, credential collection, or deceptive downloads.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
